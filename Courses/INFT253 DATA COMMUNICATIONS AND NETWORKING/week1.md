# THE LANGUAGE OF COMPUTER NETWORKS
* **Computer network**: An interconnection of computers and devices that communicate using wired or wireless media.
* Local, Metropolitan, Wide, Personal area networks.
* **Voice networks**: transmits only telephone signals.
* **Data networks**: transmits both voice and comp. data.
* **Data communications**: transfer of digital/analog data using digital/analog signals.
* **Telecommunications**: transmission of information over long distances using electronic means.
* **Network management**: design, installation, and maintenance of a network, including its hardware and software. 
* **Network cloud**: A network that stores applications, software and data.
