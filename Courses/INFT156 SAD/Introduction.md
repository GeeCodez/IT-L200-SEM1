# SYSTEM DESIGN AND ANALYSIS

**System** is an organized relationship of Inter-related components that are linked together according to plan to achieve a common cause

**System Development** is a systematic process which includes planning, analysis, design, deployment and maintenance of systems

**System Analysis** is the process of collecting and interpreting facts, identifying the problems and decomposition of a system into its components

**System Analysis** is done to study the system or its parts in order to identify its objectives

## System Design 
* Focuses on how to accomplish the objective of the system
* Is the process of planning a new system or replacing an existing by redefining its components to meets its requirements
* **SAD** Focuses on Systems Processes and Technology
