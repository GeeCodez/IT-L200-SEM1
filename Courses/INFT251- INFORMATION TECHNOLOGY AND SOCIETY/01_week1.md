## COMPUTERS
**A computer** is an electronic device that accepts data, processes it, and gives output.
**Processing** is when a computer works on data according to a program.
**Process**: is a set of logically related tasks performed to complete a task.
* Computers are: fast, reliable, accurate, versatile, consistent, automated
* They are used in: research, education, business, automation, banking, management, medicine

## INTERNET
* **Internet** is a connection of many computers via a network.
* Internet is used for: searching, sharing data, banking, ecom services, video conferencing, electronic publications.

## ICT
* **Information and Communication Technologies (ICT)** are technologies used to create, store, process, and share information.
* ICT tools include, Radio, TVs, DVDs, Telephone, satellite systems.

### COMMUNICATION 
* transferring information from one place to another. 
* Communication involves: source, encoder, transmitter, receiver, decoder, storage, retrieval and destination.
* **Synchronous**: phone calls, live chat, live meetings, etc
* **Asynchronous**: email, sms, discussion boards, mobile sms, etc

### How ICT improves education
* Online learning: zoom, coursera, etc
* Admnistrative tool: education management information systems.
* For distance learning.
* Improved access to resources.
* Enhance enquiry skills.
* Enhance communication and sharing of ideas.

### GREEN ICT
* The use of ICT in a way that reduces energy consumption, waste and environmental damage throughout its lifecycle.
* Aim: to improve environmenatal sustainability in organizations.

#### STEPS TOWARDS GREEN ICT
1. **Reduced energy consumption and CO2 emissions**
* Use of power-efficient hardware, energy-aware software design, smart power management policies.
* Impact: Cleaner energy use and low climate change

2. **Reduce environmental impact of the disposal of ICT waste products**
* Safe disposal, recycling and material recovery.
* Protects ecosystem while conserving raw materials.
3. **Reduced power consumption**
4. **Going paperless**: digital documents replacing paper system
5. **Buying energy-efficient equipments**
6. **Disposal reuse and recycling of ICT equipments**
7. **Server optimization and virtualization**
8. **Indirect ICT savings** web, tele, video conferencing reduce the use of real world energy.


### USEFUL INTERNET CONCEPTS
* **Network**: group of connected computers
* **Client**: a computer that get's information from another computer (server).
* **Protocol**: set of rules for connecting to the internet.
* **Portal**: A known gateway of the internet.
* **Router**: a networking device that connects different networks and directs traffic between them.

## IMPACTS OF ICT IN EVERYDAY LIVES
### Negative Impacts of ICT
* Reduced face-to-face interaction -> Introversion
* Social disconnect despite online connections
* High cost of gadgets, causing unequal accessibilty
* Job Loss due to automation
* Security risks, privacy loss and online fraud
* Health problems.

### Positive Impacts of ICT.
* Reduces paper usage
* Improved health literacy through online health info access.
* Entertainment
* Education 
* Fast information sharing
* Maintaining social relationships.
