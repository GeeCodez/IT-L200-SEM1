# CRITICAL ANALISIS AND EVALUATION OF TEXT


### STEPS IN ANALISING A TEXT
* Give it a description ( an overview of the text)
* Identify the context ( background or sorrounding information of the text)
* Interpretation - putting what you read into your own words
* Evaluation - compare, contrast and extract multiple viewpoints whiles avoiding biases. 
 - Evaluation weighs the credibility of the owner, it examines the accuracy and suggests whether it reasonable and valid
* Conclusion
