**Assests** are things that can bring profit and help the business to operate effectively

**Liabilities** are the financial obligations that the business is supposed to meet and they don't bring any profit to the business.

**Business transaction** is an event which involves the transfer of money or money's worth. business transaction is concluded when money is paid.
