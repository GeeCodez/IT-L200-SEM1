**Accounting**: is the process of recording, classifying, summarizing, analyzing, interpreting and reporting the financial transactions of a business. 

### Accounting informationn is relevant to:
* Management
* Shareholders 
* Creditors
* Customers
* Employees 
* Gov't 

**Debtors** are those who owe and are supposed to pay to their **creditors**

#### Financial accounting concerns with:
* Revenue
* Expenses
* Assets
* Liabilities
* 

**Assests** are things that can bring profit and help the business to operate effectively

**Liabilities** are the financial obligations that the business is supposed to meet and they don't bring any profit to the business.

**Business transaction** is an event which involves the transfer of what money's worth. business transaction is concluded when money is paid.
